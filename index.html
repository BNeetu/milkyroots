Our Story

The Bhati Family Legacy

From a humble polyhouse to a thriving pure dairy farm — this is the story of passion, purity and the Bhati family's unwavering commitment to natural, honest food.

The Bhati family of Beawar, Rajasthan has always believed that the best food comes from the earth — grown with care, raised with love, and delivered with honesty. Their journey began not with dairy, but with soil.

Years ago, the Bhati family established their polyhouse farm — a climate-controlled greenhouse where they grew a wide range of fresh, organic farm products for the people of Beawar. From juicy tomatoes and leafy spinach to brinjal, capsicum and seasonal greens — every vegetable was grown without chemicals or pesticides, using only natural compost and traditional farming wisdom passed down through generations.

The polyhouse became a trusted name in Beawar. Families who tasted the Bhati farm products knew the difference — the flavour, the freshness, the purity that only comes from land that is loved and respected.

Inspired by this success and driven by a deeper purpose, the Bhati family took their next bold step — establishing MilkyRoots, a pure desi cow dairy farm. The same values that made their farm products exceptional now guide every drop of milk: zero adulteration, zero shortcuts, and complete transparency from farm to your kitchen table.

Today, MilkyRoots stands as Beawar's most trusted pure dairy — carrying forward the Bhati family's lifetime of farming heritage, with a proud commitment to feeding the community the way nature intended.

Our Polyhouse — Where It All Began

The Bhati family's polyhouse is a modern greenhouse farm where farm products are grown in a controlled, chemical-free environment. Temperature, humidity and water are carefully managed to produce the healthiest, most nutritious farm products year-round.

  • 100% organic — no pesticides, no chemicals
  • Natural compost and traditional fertilisers only
  • Year-round availability of seasonal farm products
  • Tomatoes, spinach, brinjal, capsicum, methi, coriander and more
  • Supplied to families directly in Beawar
🐄

Our Desi Cows — The Heart of MilkyRoots

We chose pure desi Indian cows — India's most prized indigenous breed — for a very good reason. Our cows naturally produce A2 beta-casein milk, which is far easier to digest, richer in nutrients, and free from the inflammatory proteins found in foreign Holstein-Friesian cows.

  • Pure desi Indian breed
  • Raised free-range on natural green fodder (green rachka, green janwar, and green Bajra)
  • No hormonal elements or antibiotics — ever
  • Hand-milked by experienced gawalas at 4 AM daily
  • Happy cows, healthy calves, purer milk — always
🥛

Milk Purity — Our Absolute Promise

At MilkyRoots, milk purity is not a marketing claim — it is a way of life. Every batch of milk goes through a strict quality check before it leaves our farm.

  • Fat content: natural 4.0–4.5% — never diluted
  • Zero water mixing — ever
  • No milk powder, no preservatives
  • No synthetic hormones or oxytocin injections

Our Farm — A Living Ecosystem

MilkyRoots is not just a dairy — it is a complete farm ecosystem where cows, calves, farm products and the earth all thrive together. The cows' natural waste feeds the polyhouse plants. The farm grows fodder for the cows. Nothing is wasted.

  • Integrated farm — dairy + polyhouse working together
  • Cow dung used as organic fertiliser for farm products
