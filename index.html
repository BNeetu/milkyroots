Our Polyhouse — Where It All Began

The Bhati family's polyhouse is a modern greenhouse farm where farm products are grown in a controlled, chemical-free environment. Temperature, humidity and water are carefully managed to produce the healthiest, most nutritious farm products year-round.

  • 100% organic — no pesticides, no chemicals
  • Natural compost and traditional fertilisers only
  • Year-round availability of seasonal farm products
  • Tomatoes, spinach, brinjal, capsicum, methi, coriander and more
  • Supplied to families directly in Beawar

Our Desi Cows — The Heart of Bhati Farms

We chose pure desi Indian cows — India's most prized indigenous breed — for a very good reason. Our cows naturally produce A2 beta-casein milk, which is far easier to digest, richer in nutrients, and free from the inflammatory proteins found in foreign Holstein-Friesian cows.

  • Pure desi Indian breed
  • Raised free-range on natural green fodder (green rachka, green janwar, and green Bajra)
  • No hormonal elements or antibiotics — ever
  • Hand-milked by experienced gawalas at 4 AM daily
  • Happy cows, healthy calves, purer milk — always
🥛

Milk Purity — Our Absolute Promise

At Bhati Farms, milk purity is not a marketing claim — it is a way of life. Every batch of milk goes through a strict quality check before it leaves our farm.

  • Fat content: natural 4.0–4.5% — never diluted
  • Zero water mixing — ever
  • No milk powder, no preservatives
  • No synthetic hormones or oxytocin injections
  • Fresh within 2 hours of milking — delivered as early as possible (we will try our best to deliver up to 7 o'clock; in rare situations beyond our control, such as natural or climatic conditions, slight delays may occur).

Our Farm — A Living Ecosystem

Bhati Farms is not just a dairy — it is a complete farm ecosystem where cows, calves, farm products and the earth all thrive together. The cows' natural waste feeds the polyhouse plants. The farm grows fodder for the cows. Nothing is wasted.

  • Integrated farm — dairy + polyhouse working together
  • Cow dung used as organic fertiliser for farm products
  • Natural fodder — bajra, jowar, fresh green grass
  • Clean, spacious gau shaala — welfare first
  • Located in the fertile belt near Beawar, Rajasthan

Is your milk really pure? How can I trust it?
Yes — 100%. Our cows are raised on our own farm in Beawar. Milking starts at 4 AM, quality-checked for fat and purity, and delivered to you (we will try our best to deliver up to 7 o'clock; in rare situations beyond our control, such as natural or climatic conditions, slight delays may occur).
